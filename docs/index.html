We Are Hot Beans Web

Hot Beans Web was founded in 2020 by Bob Bean. During COVID, when people transitioned to working from home and many of the population were placed on furlough, there was plenty of free time. Bob noticed people taking on personal web related projects covering a range of subjects and interests. With his expertise in web development, he wished he could step in to speed up the process for those taking on personal projects. He quickly gathered a team and advertised through social media channels. It proved a great move as jobs started to pour in, and they have been ever since. Hot Beans Web serves up extremely well-designed, bespoke websites, using cutting edge technologies. All staff are well versed in the latest design and development trends, and this shows in the final products. We provide services to clients worldwide, focussing solely on them which gives assurances that they are the only one. Truly bespoke! You can meet some of our team here.

Our Mission: “Growing your website from the ground up.”

Our goal at Hot Beans Web is to make truly memorable and bespoke websites that cater to your every need. Let's chat!
